Xing Yi Movie

In this project, my partner Xing Yang and I were able to use Replit to create a webpage by applying our previous understanding of the basic CSS (Cascading Style Sheets) and HTML (Hyper Text Markup Language). My partner's amazing imagination came into play as we created a fictional, attention-grabbing plot for the Movie-Webpage! After that, we divided up the requirements given in the rubric and tackled the tasks one by one.

During the creation of our Movie-Webpage, I believe we did a great job of supporting one another when we were creating our movie webpage. I didn't hesitate to ask my partner questions if I came across something difficult. One thing that personally challenged me was making typos in the programming that resulted in the code to fail. I now understand how important it is to review our code for errors. I've learned the value of communication through this project; without Slack, it would have been extremely difficult to contact my partner. We completed the main requirements and then created a sequel for fun. I was able to organize my learning through this assignment, which was a huge accomplishment for me. I am looking forward to working with my classmates and creating projects like this!
