SEP10 Freedom Project

The "Freedom Project" for SEP10 is a year-long project all about making a website that informs the viewer of the current and future innovations in the topic of my choosing. For my project, I chose the topic of fashion. I chose this topic because I have a passion for fashion. I learned many new things during my research that I had to do for this project.

In order to make this project complete, I used HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and Github. I also chose to independently study Wow.Js and Animate.css in order to help me make by website. I did go through alot of challenges along the way, but I overcame them. As I worked on this year-long project, I wrote blogs so you can see my process. I made sure to follow my EDP (Engineering Design Process) and jot down everything that I needed. I gained several skills along this journey that I am so proud of. In the future, I will be sure to use these skills that I gained and learn many more hopefully!
